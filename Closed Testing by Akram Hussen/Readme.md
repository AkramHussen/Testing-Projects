
# Closed Testing for Google Play Apps

This project showcases the workflow used for **closed testing Android apps on Google Play Console**, focusing on release readiness and real user feedback before going public.

---

## Service Overview

I provide a professional closed testing  for Google Play apps.  
The goal is to validate the app with real testers, collect meaningful feedback, and help ensure the app is ready for public release.

---

## How the Closed Test Is Run

- Set up and manage a closed testing track on Google Play Console.
- Invite and coordinate a group of real testers to install and use the app.
- Monitor usage during the test period and collect feedback on performance, stability, and user experience.
- Highlight issues that should be fixed before enabling the ‚ÄúProduction‚Äù / public release button.

---

## Scope & Limitations

- The service covers **closed testing only** (no development or publishing on behalf of the owner).
- The app owner is responsible for complying with **Google Play policies** and any content or policy violations.
- Apps that clearly violate Google Play policies are not accepted.
- Account or app removals caused by policy violations or external reasons are outside the scope of this service.

---

## What You Receive

- A short **closed testing summary report** (test period, number of testers, key findings).
- A list of the **most important issues and improvement suggestions** from testers.
---

## Required Information to Start

To start a closed test for your app, please provide:

- Link to the app in **Closed Testing** mode on Google Play Console (or invitation instructions).
- Any specific areas or flows you want testers to focus on (optional but recommended).
